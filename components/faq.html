FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ShopFlow.

Does it support barcode scanners?
Yes, all standard USB and Bluetooth barcode scanners work seamlessly with ShopFlow. Plug and play — no additional drivers needed.
Does it support thermal printers?
Absolutely. We support all major thermal receipt and label printers from brands like Epson, Star, and Zebra.
Is GST billing supported?
Yes, full GST compliance with customizable invoice templates, HSN/SAC codes, and detailed GST reports.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes, cloud sync lets you access your data from any device — desktop, laptop, tablet, or mobile. All data stays in sync.
Is my data secure?
Bank-grade encryption (AES-256), daily automated backups, and secure cloud infrastructure keep your business data safe.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your data will remain available for 30 days after cancellation.
Do you provide customer support?
24/7 priority support via email, live chat, and phone. Our team is always ready to help you succeed.
